===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And, you fathers, do not provoke your children to anger** - "You fathers must not do things that will make your children angry" or "You fathers must not cause your children to be angry"
  * **raise them in the discipline and instruction of the Lord** - "bring them up in the training and teaching of the Lord"
